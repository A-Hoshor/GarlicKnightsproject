Interview One:

Interview Date: 1/14/2023

Interviewer: Lawrence C.

Interviewee: Morrie F.

Question: What is your approximate age

Answer: 25

Question: What kind of books do you enjoy reading?

Answer: I like variety! I enjoy reading non-fiction, psychology, self-help, research, and job specific books.

Question: How often do you read?

Answer: Intermittently if I'm being honest! I will read everyday to finish a book and then go weeks without reading.

Question: Could you tell me a little bit about your education background, what you do for a career, and a little bit about your family life?

Answer: Certainly! Well, I have a Bachelors in Marketing, and I also have a Masters in Business Administration. I work fulltime remotely for the government, and I'm married with a child that's under ten years old!

Question: Do you have any reading applications that you use?

Answer: Yes, I use the Kindle app on my phone.

Question: What is your experience level with technology?

Answer: I would say I'm pretty experienced. I use technology everyday for work and personal use, such as GPS, social media, healthcare, and banking apps.

Question: What would you use a book recommendation app for?

Answer: I would use it to find books that I would enjoy reading and to find books that I could recommend to my friends and family. Although at the moment, I don't have a specific book in mind.
